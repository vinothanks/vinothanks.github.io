Features

Car Parking

Ample amount of parking space in front of the cottages, to accomodate medium to large size vehicle.

Kitchen

Cottages come with attached Kitchen. Guests can use them to prepare meals. Extra charges for gas,vessels,cook.

Hall

A Common Hall is available in all the cottages, apart from the rooms. With tv, chair and sofa.

We are pet Friendly. Damage to property by pet, has to be compensated by the owners.

Bonfire can be arranged in front of the cottages at night, to warm up and have fun. Extra charges applicable.

Room boys will be available at all time for your assistance.
